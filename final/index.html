Welcome to Anytown, USA Shooting Gallery!

How to Play

Shoot the targets to earn points.

Shooting the ducks will cost you points.

Controls

Use WASD to move.

Use Left and Right arrows to rotate.

Press SPACE to shoot.

Cheats

Free Movement

Allow free movement with the use of ↑ ↓ arrows.

Bullet Speed

Speed your bullets travel.

Reload Speed

Speed of your gun's reload time.

Duck & Target Speed

Movement speed of the ducks and targets.

Walking Speed

Your walking speed. This feature is experimental.
